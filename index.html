Why Most Investors Miss Discovery Opportunities

Manual Monitoring Misses 90% of Announcements

With over 500 daily ASX releases, even dedicated investors can't manually track every drilling result. Critical discoveries often occur outside market hours or during busy periods when attention is divided elsewhere.

Can't Compare Different Commodities Effectively

A lithium intercept in ppm looks nothing like a gold result in g/t. Without standardized conversion, investors stick to familiar commodities and miss major opportunities in sectors they don't fully understand.

React Too Late After Price Movements Begin

By the time drilling results appear in market commentary or social media, early price movements have often already occurred. The biggest gains go to those who act on raw data, not processed opinions.
